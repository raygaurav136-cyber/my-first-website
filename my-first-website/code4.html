How food affects your mind and habits:

1.Food becomes brain fuel:-

Whatever you eat is broken down into nutrients(like glucose, vitamins, minerals).

Your brain uses this as energy to think, focus, and control emotions.

  • good fuel → smooth working mind

  • Bad fuel → slow, irritated mind

2.Food affects brain chemicals (neurotransmitters):-

Your mood depends on chemicals like:

  • serotonin → happiness & calmness

  • Dopamine → motivation & reward

    • These chemicals are made from the food you eat.


  • Healthy food → balanced chemicals → stable mood

  • Junk food → imbalance → mood swings, irritation

3.Blood sugar impact

Food controls your blood sugar level, which directly affects your mind.

  • Stable sugar (fruits, roti, dal) → steady energy & focus

  • Sudden spikes (sugar, junk food) → energy crash → laziness & irritation

4.Gut-brain connection

Your stomach (gut) and brain are connected.

    If your digestion is good:

  • Mind feels light and calm

    • If digestion is bed:

  • You feel heavy, irritated, distracted
