Benefits of calm mind:

  1. Better focus → You can concentrate on studies or work without distraction.

  2. Good decision making → You think clearly and make smarter choices.

  3. Less stress and anxiety → Your mind stays relaxed, so you feel less tension.

  4. Emotional control → You don't get angry or upset easily.

  5. Better relationships → You talk politely and understand others better.

  6. Spiritual growth → A peaceful mind helps in meditation, prayer, and connection with God.

Higher state of mind means:-
